Prompt Ladder: Build the Answer Slowly

If I cannot answer immediately, I do not panic. I answer in steps.

🪜 1

Ladder 1: Fact

What is true in my life?

💬 Example: "I live in a house."
🪜 2

Ladder 2: Detail

Add one real detail.

💬 Example: "It has two floors and a small garden."
🪜 3

Ladder 3: Feeling

How do I feel about it?

💬 Example: "It feels comfortable for my family."
🪜 4

Ladder 4: Final answer

Put it together simply.

💬 Example: "I live in a house with two floors and a small garden. It is not very big, but it feels comfortable for my family."

PAGE 9 - REFERENCE

Van's Speaking Repair List

💡 Use this page before recording homework or before the next lesson.

🎯

1. Clean endings

When my idea is finished, I stop.

❌ Bad:

I live in Viet Tri. Yeah.

✅ Better:

I live in Viet Tri, in Phu Tho province.

❌ Bad:

My favourite room is the living room. Yeah.

✅ Better:

My favourite room is the living room because I can spend time with my family there.

💬

2. If I forget a word

I do not need to stop.
I can say:

“I don't know the exact English word, but it is a kind of...”
  • I don't know the exact English word, but it is a kind of small garden.
  • I don't know the exact English word, but it is a kind of family room.
  • I don't know the exact English word, but it is a place where my family spends time together.
✏️

3. Useful self-correction phrase

If I make a mistake, I can fix it.

“What I mean is...”
  • My families - what I mean is, my family - often spends time in the living room.
💬

4. Small phrases I can use today

  • I currently live in...
  • It is located in...
  • It is also my hometown.
  • I live in a house with two floors.
  • There is a small garden.
  • My favourite room is the living room.
  • I can spend time with my family there.
  • It feels lively.
  • I don't meet my neighbours very often.
  • From what my parents say, they are friendly.
  • One thing I don't really like is the traffic.
  • It is not very convenient to travel around the city.
⭐

5. Final reminder

Do not try to sound advanced.
Try to sound:

“clear, specific, truthful, and controlled”

That is enough for today.
